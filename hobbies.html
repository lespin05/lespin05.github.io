Who am I, for real this time.

Howdy, My name's Luis Espinoza, I'm 19 years old, soon to be 20 years old, and come from Ciudad Juarez, Mexico, and live in the United States to get a better education, and I guess a better life, or at least that's what they say, now with how things are going today, things migth get harder, maybe even harder because of my Mexican Roots? who knows...

Here at NMSU, I'm taking the Major of Computer Science because of my curiosity to learn more about how these systems work. It's been a great and challenging journey, but I guess learning new things along the way cannot hurt someone. It has been a bit tiring, given some of my classes, and subjects I have to learn have been difficult, and sometimes giving homework over and over and over again that it's starting to feel like a cycle. But other than that, my experience here at NMSU has been great and fun, still with the sub-goal of trying to make more friends around, despite having a lot of difficulty speaking to people.

Hobbies - The things that keep me chill

There's two hobbies I do everyday, and both are my personal favorites, those bing drawing and videogames.

Drawing (Not in professional manners)

I've been drawing for as long as when I was a small lad, but haven't flourished much of my drawings until I around like Middle School or High School. From there, I've picked more and more on drawing until it just became a passtime to do when I have time to spare, or an idea flourishes to my mind.
